George Lang's Potato Bread Recipe With Caraway Seeds

Easy vegetable broth made right at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large onion
  • 4 - 5 Large Carrots
  • 1 Bunch of Celery
  • 1 Head of Garlic
  • 1 Bunch Fresh Thyme
  • 2 Dried bay leaves
  • About 10 whole pepper corns.

Directions

  1. Wash all vegetables and the fresh herbs well.
  2. Do not peel ANY of the vegetables! The skins will add a beautiful flavor and color to the broth.
  3. Prep the vegetables:
    • Quarter the onions.
    • Chop the carrots into 2 to 3 inch pieces.
    • Seperate the celery stalks and chop into 3 to 4 inch pieces.
    • Carefully cut the garlic across the equater so that you cut each bulb in half.
    • The washed herbs are good to go as is.
  4. Add all ingredients to a pot.
  5. Add enough water to cover all of the vegetables by about an inch.
  6. Heat the pot over medium high heat, stirring often to prevent burning, until it reaches a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat to maintain a simmer.
  8. Cover and simmer for 2 to 3 hours, until the vegetables are very tender and the broth and is fragrant.
  9. Carfully remove the vegetables, making sure to strain any retained broth using the back of a spoon.
  10. Seperate the broth into smaller containers for storage.
  11. Store in the fridge.
